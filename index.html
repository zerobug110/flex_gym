our story

Flex gym was establish in 2011 in the gambia mainly to help worker who have very less to take care of their own health. Flex gym first started with only night session because that the only time workers are free to work out. we have seven professional trainers. A trainer can be assign to a person if only they subscribe to the premium membership. we also have 55 members as of at now.
